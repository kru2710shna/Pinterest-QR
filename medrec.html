💊 CureTrack – Personalized Medical Recommendation

A web platform for symptom-based health suggestions

Overview

CureTrack is a web-based platform designed to help individuals manage their health by providing symptom-based recommendations for medication, diet, workout routines, and precautions. It also delivers disease information and downloadable prescriptions in PDF format.

Key Features

  • 🩺 Symptom-Based Recommendations: Tailored suggestions for medication, diet, and workouts.
  • 💊 Medication Suggestions: Advice on appropriate medications for reported symptoms.
  • 🥗 Dietary Plans: Custom nutrition plans to complement medical treatment.
  • 🏃 Workout Routines: Personalized exercises for symptom management.
  • ⚠️ Precautions: Guidance to avoid worsening health conditions.
  • 📚 Disease Information: Detailed descriptions of possible conditions.
  • 📄 Downloadable PDF: Prescription and recommendations export.

Technologies Used

  • Front-End: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Back-End: Flask + Gunicorn, Python-dotenv
  • Programming: Python
  • Data Analysis: NumPy, Matplotlib, SciPy, Joblib, Threadpoolctl
  • EDA: Image and text analysis
  • ML & DL: Scikit-learn, SVM
  • Deployment: Render
  • PDF Generation: FPDF
  • Other Tools: Jinja2, Click, Blinker, Itsdangerous
